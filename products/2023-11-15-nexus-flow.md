A project management tool designed for high-velocity engineering teams. **NexusFlow** integrates directly with your version control to provide real-time updates on task progress without manual entry.

It's not just about moving tickets; it's about flow.

![Dashboard Preview](https://picsum.photos/seed/nexus-dashboard/800/400)

### Key Features
*   **Automated Status Updates**: Commits and PRs automatically move tickets.
*   **Velocity Tracking**: Real-time burn-down charts that actually make sense.
*   **Focus Mode**: A simplified view for engineers that hides the noise.
